# Synapse vs IsotopeAI - Feature Comparison

## Overview

### Synapse
- AI-powered study platform for PUC students (Science & Commerce streams)

- Built-in admin panel for content management
- Focus on Indian curriculum (JEE, NEET, Boards, Commerce)

### IsotopeAI
- AI-powered study planner, focus timer, progress tracking, and analytics

3. **Dedicated Exam Tracker**: Countdowns, syllabus management, OCR syllabus extraction, weak topic tracking
4. **Built-in Admin Panel**: Content management for tests and syllabus
5. **Commerce Stream Support**: Full support for Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, Statistics
6. **Highly Customizable UI**: Draggable widgets, background images, theme options
7. **Social Features**: Groups and collaboration
8. **Multiple OCR Use Cases**: Note scanning and syllabus extraction
